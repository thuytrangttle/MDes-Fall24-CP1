Proposal for Interactive Experience: "Emoji Face-Off"

Emoj Face-Off challenges players to solve riddles and pick the emoji that best answers or represents the scenario described in the riddle. Players can compete friends to see who can react fastest and most accurately to the riddles. Each riddle will describe a scenario, and players must choose the emoji that best fits the clue.

How it Works: Players are presented with a riddle or a short description of a situation. The player needs to quickly guess the emoji that best represents the riddle.
